Welcome to CCA

Climate and Communities Alliance is a Kenyan-based NGO dedicated to creating sustainable, climate-resilient communities. Join us in building a greener, more sustainable future for Kenya.

About Us

Mission: Empower Kenyan communities by promoting sustainable environmental practices and strengthening resilience to climate impacts.

Vision: A Kenya where communities thrive with knowledge and tools to combat climate change.

Our Programs

  • Environmental Education: Promoting environmental awareness and sustainable practices.
  • Climate-Resilient Agriculture: Training farmers to adopt climate-smart techniques.
  • Advocacy and Policy Development: Working with governments to promote environmental sustainability.
  • Reforestation and Conservation: Restoring degraded lands to combat deforestation.
  • Water Resource Management: Ensuring access to clean water for all communities.
